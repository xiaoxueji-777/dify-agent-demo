<div class="challenge-card">

<div class="challenge-row">

<div class="challenge-label label-problem">问题</div>

<div class="challenge-content">

HTTP 请求报错 <strong>`ascii codec can't encode`</strong>，Supabase 连接失败

</div>

</div>

<div class="challenge-row">

<div class="challenge-label label-root">根源</div>

<div class="challenge-content">

</div>

</div>

<div class="challenge-row">

<div class="challenge-label label-solve">解决</div>

<div class="challenge-content">

使用"文本还原器"工具清理字符 + 记事本中转纯手工输入 + <strong>重新生成 Supabase 密钥</strong>

</div>

</div>

</div>


<div class="insight-box" style="border-color:rgba(252,129,129,0.2);background:rgba(252,129,129,0.06);color:#fc8181;">

</div>

</div>
